Our Story

A garden
tended by family

Secret Gardens is more than a salon — it's a family legacy rooted in care, craft, and community. Nestled in the charming Danish village of Solvang, our doors have always been open with warmth and a personal touch that only family can offer.

Our grandmother brought the vision. Our mother built upon it. Together, they have cultivated a space where every guest is treated like a dear friend — where you can sit down, breathe, and leave feeling truly cared for.

From precision cuts to color artistry, nails, and skincare, every service we offer is delivered with the love and expertise that only comes from years of passion for this craft.
